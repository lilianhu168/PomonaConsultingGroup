We Strive for Excellence

Real value for organizations. Real experience for students.

Pomona Consulting Group is a student organization at the Claremont Colleges focused on bringing real value to a variety of businesses. We support pre-professional development for students through hands-on project experience, networking events, and workshops. Our former clients have ranged from startups to community groups and Fortune 500 organizations.
