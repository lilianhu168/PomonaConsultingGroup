PCG pipeline

A semester-based consulting journey.

Most students begin with Associate Consultant training, then move into consultant project work, and later step into project leadership.

01 Associate Consultant

Explore consulting foundations.

A semester-long learning experience with weekly workshops, a real client case study, speaker events, coffee chats, and networking opportunities.

02 Consultant

Apply skills to a client project.

Consultants work in teams of three to four under a Project Lead on a semester-long project for a real client.

03 Project Lead

Own the client-facing work.

Project Leads manage client relationships, guide consultant teams, and drive final recommendations from scoping through presentation.
