Student experience

Accessible, applied, and team-based.

Students can join PCG through training, project teams, and internal leadership. The goal is to make consulting feel less distant: you learn the tools, practice with peers, and apply them to real client questions.

Learn

Consulting foundations

Build comfort with problem framing, research, synthesis, slides, client communication, and team workflows.

Apply

Real project work

Join teams that translate ambiguous client questions into research, insights, and actionable recommendations.

Lead

Organizational ownership

Grow into project lead, internal, external, marketing, operations, or recruiting responsibilities.
